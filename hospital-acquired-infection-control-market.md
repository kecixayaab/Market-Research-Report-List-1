Central Line-associated Bloodstream Infections (CLABSIs) are related to the use of central venous catheters, warranting precise insertion and maintenance techniques. Respiratory Infections emerge through ventilation equipment, highlighting the need for rigorous hygiene protocols and surveillance.</p></p>
