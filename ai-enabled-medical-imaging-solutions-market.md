<p><p>AI-enabled medical imaging solutions are categorized into three market types: On-premise, Cloud-Based, and Web-Based. On-premise solutions are installed and operated within the healthcare facility, offering enhanced control but requiring significant IT resources. Cloud-Based solutions utilize remote servers to store and analyze imaging data, providing scalability and easier access but raising potential security concerns. Web-Based solutions allow for direct access through internet browsers, facilitating collaboration and accessibility across different platforms, while often relying on cloud infrastructure for data management.</p></p>
