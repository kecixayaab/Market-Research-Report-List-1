<p><ul><li>Horizontal Furnace</li><li>Vertical Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor processing furnace market is primarily divided into horizontal and vertical furnace types. Horizontal furnaces allow for efficient wafer loading and uniform processing by placing wafers flat, which optimizes heat distribution for applications like oxidation and diffusion. Vertical furnaces, on the other hand, utilize a vertical orientation, streamlining space and enhancing efficiency, particularly suitable for batch processing with large substrates. Both types cater to specific manufacturing needs, impacting production efficiency and yield in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
