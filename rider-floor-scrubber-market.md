<p><p>The rider floor scrubber market features three main types: disk head, cylindrical head, and square oscillating head. Disk head scrubbers utilize a circular pad that effectively cleans large, flat surfaces, making them ideal for open areas. Cylindrical head scrubbers employ rotating brushes that agitate and scrub surfaces, excelling at handling uneven terrain and tough debris. Square oscillating head models offer versatility with their oscillating motion, enabling a thorough clean in corners and edges while providing consistent pressure across various surfaces.</p></p>
