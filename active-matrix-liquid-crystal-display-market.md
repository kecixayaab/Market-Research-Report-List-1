<p><p>The Active Matrix Liquid Crystal Display (AMLCD) market encompasses different types of technologies, primarily Polycrystalline Silicon (poly-Si) and Amorphous Silicon (a-Si). Polycrystalline silicon offers faster response times and higher resolution, making it suitable for high-end applications like televisions and high-resolution monitors. In contrast, amorphous silicon is more cost-effective and typically used in lower-resolution displays, such as smartphones and basic tablets. Both types cater to varying consumer needs based on performance, cost, and application preferences.</p></p>
