<p><strong>The Artificial Larynx Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neck Type</li><li>Intraoral Type</li><li>Electromyography (EMG) Transduce</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial larynx market encompasses three primary types: neck type, intraoral type, and electromyography (EMG) transducer. The neck type devices, worn externally, generate sound through vibration, making them suitable for patients with laryngeal conditions. Intraoral types are designed for placement within the mouth, offering a more discreet option for voice production. EMG transducers utilize electrical signals from throat muscles to create speech, enhancing communication for those who have lost their natural voice. Each type addresses specific user needs and preferences.</p></p>
