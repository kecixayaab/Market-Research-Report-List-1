<p><p>The sideshaft CV joint market primarily consists of two types: outboard and inboard joints. Outboard joints are typically located near the wheel hub, delivering power to the wheels while accommodating steering movements. In contrast, inboard joints are positioned closer to the transmission, allowing for flexibility in the driveshaft and connection to the engine. Both types are crucial for efficient power transmission in vehicles, enabling smooth operation and improved handling in various driving conditions.</p></p>
