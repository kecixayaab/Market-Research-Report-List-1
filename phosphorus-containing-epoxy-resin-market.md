<p><strong>The Phosphorus-Containing Epoxy Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Reaction</li><li>Physical Addition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphorus-containing epoxy resin market can be categorized into two main types based on their formation processes: chemical reaction and physical addition. </p><p>In the chemical reaction type, phosphorus compounds chemically bond with epoxy resins during curing, enhancing flame retardancy and thermal stability. </p><p>On the other hand, the physical addition type involves incorporating phosphorus-containing additives into the epoxy formulation without altering their chemical structure. This method also improves fire resistance while maintaining the desired properties of the base resin.</p></p>
