<p><p>The automotive safety device market consists of two primary segments: active safety systems and passive safety systems. Active safety systems are designed to prevent accidents and enhance vehicle control through technologies like anti-lock braking systems, electronic stability control, and advanced driver-assistance systems, which assist drivers in real-time. Conversely, passive safety systems focus on minimizing injuries during an accident, including features such as airbags, seatbelts, and crumple zones that absorb impact energy and protect occupants. Together, these systems enhance overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
