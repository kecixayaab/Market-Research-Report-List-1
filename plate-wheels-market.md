<p><p>Plate wheels are categorized into three main types based on their design and application: Simplex, Duplex, and Triplex plate wheels. Simplex plate wheels consist of a single plate, typically used for basic lifting tasks. Duplex plate wheels feature two plates and offer enhanced strength and stability, making them suitable for heavier loads. Triplex plate wheels have three plates, providing even greater durability and load-bearing capacity, ideal for more demanding industrial applications. Each type serves specific operational needs within the material handling market.</p></p>
