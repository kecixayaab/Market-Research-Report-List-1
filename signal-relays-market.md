<p><p>The signal relays market is categorized into DC and AC signal relays. In contrast, AC signal relays function with alternating current, commonly used in industrial and electrical equipment due to their ability to manage higher voltage levels. Both types are essential for controlling circuits, offering reliable switching capabilities and enabling efficient signal management across various industries.</p></p>
