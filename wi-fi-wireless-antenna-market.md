<p><ul><li>Omni directional antennas</li><li>Directional Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wi-Fi wireless antenna market comprises two main types: omnidirectional and directional antennas. Omnidirectional antennas distribute signals in a 360-degree pattern, making them ideal for general coverage in open areas, such as homes or offices. They provide consistent connectivity but may have reduced range and signal strength over distance. In contrast, directional antennas focus the signal in a specific direction, enhancing range and throughput for targeted applications, such as connecting distant devices or extending coverage in large spaces.</p></p>
