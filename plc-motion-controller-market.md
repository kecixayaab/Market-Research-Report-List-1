<p><ul><li>Compact Controller</li><li>Modular Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PLC motion controller market is categorized into two primary types: compact controllers and modular controllers. Compact controllers are integrated units that combine motion control and PLC functions in a single device, suitable for smaller applications with limited space. They offer cost-effectiveness and ease of use. In contrast, modular controllers consist of separate modules for different functions, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability in complex systems. This makes them ideal for large, intricate automation projects where customization and expansion are essential.</p></p>
