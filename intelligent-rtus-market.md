<p><p>The Intelligent RTU (Remote Terminal Unit) market consists of two primary types: Wireless RTUs and Wired RTUs. Wireless RTUs utilize cellular, satellite, or radio communication to transmit data, offering flexibility and ease of installation, particularly in remote or hard-to-access locations. In contrast, Wired RTUs connect directly through physical cables, providing stable, high-speed data transmission, often preferred in environments where reliability and security are paramount. Both types play crucial roles in monitoring and controlling industrial processes efficiently.</p></p>
