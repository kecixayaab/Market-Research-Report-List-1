<p><strong>The LCD Backlight Unit BLU Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edge Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) market is divided into two primary types: Edge Type and Direct Type. Edge Type BLUs utilize LEDs placed along the edges of the screen, allowing for thinner display designs and energy efficiency, making them ideal for slim devices. In contrast, Direct Type BLUs feature LEDs positioned directly behind the LCD panel, providing even light distribution and enhanced color accuracy, which is beneficial for larger screens and professional applications requiring superior image quality.</p></p>
