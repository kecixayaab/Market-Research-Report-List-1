<p><ul><li>Single Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fishing sensor market can be categorized into single frequency and dual frequency types. Single frequency sensors operate at one frequency, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for basic fishing needs. In contrast, dual frequency sensors operate at two frequencies, providing enhanced depth tracking and improved target discrimination. This allows for more precise underwater imaging, helping anglers pinpoint specific species and structures. Both types serve different user needs, from recreational fishermen to professional anglers seeking advanced technology.</p></p>
