<p><p>The gene deletion vaccine market includes two primary types: single gene deletion vaccines and double gene deletion vaccines. Single gene deletion vaccines target the removal of one specific gene from a pathogen's genome, reducing its virulence while maintaining immune recognition. In contrast, double gene deletion vaccines eliminate two genes, enhancing efficacy and safety by further attenuating the pathogen. Both types aim to stimulate an immune response without causing disease, making them promising options for various infectious diseases.</p></p>
