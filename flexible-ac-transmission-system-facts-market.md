<p><ul><li>Shunt Compensation</li><li>Series Compensation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) enhance the control and stability of electrical power transmission networks. The market is primarily divided into two types: shunt compensation and series compensation. Shunt compensation involves devices like static var compensators that regulate voltage and improve power factor by providing or absorbing reactive power. Series compensation uses devices such as static synchronous series compensators to control system impedance, thereby increasing transmission capability and stability by dynamically managing power flow and minimizing losses.</p></p>
