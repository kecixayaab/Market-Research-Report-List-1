<p><p>The Power Steering Cylinder market is primarily divided into two types: Hydraulic Power Steering Cylinders and Electric Power Steering Cylinders. Hydraulic Power Steering Cylinders utilize fluid pressure to assist in steering, offering enhanced control and responsiveness, making them popular in traditional vehicles. In contrast, Electric Power Steering Cylinders use an electric motor to provide assistance, promoting energy efficiency and lighter weight, thus gaining traction in modern vehicles. Both types play crucial roles in improving driving comfort and safety.</p></p>
