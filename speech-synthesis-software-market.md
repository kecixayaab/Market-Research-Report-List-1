<p><strong>The Speech Synthesis Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concatenation Synthesis</li><li>Formant Synthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Speech synthesis software market encompasses various types, primarily concatenation synthesis and formant synthesis. Concatenation synthesis involves piecing together segments of recorded speech, resulting in natural-sounding audio but requiring extensive databases. In contrast, formant synthesis generates speech by simulating vocal tract dynamics using algorithms, allowing for real-time generation but often resulting in a less natural sound. Both techniques cater to different applications, influencing user experience and functionality in voice assistants, navigation systems, and accessibility tools.</p></p>
