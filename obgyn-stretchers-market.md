<p><p>The OB/GYN stretchers market consists of three main types: fixed stretchers, adjustable stretchers, and stretcher chairs. Fixed stretchers are designed for stable, unmovable use, providing a reliable option for transporting patients. Adjustable stretchers offer flexibility in height and positioning, catering to various clinical needs and improving patient comfort. Stretcher chairs combine the functionalities of a stretcher and a chair, allowing for easy patient transfer in different environments, particularly suitable for outpatient settings or transitional care.</p></p>
