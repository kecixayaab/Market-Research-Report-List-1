<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-hole Technology</li><li>Surface-mount Technology</li><li>Plug-in Technology</li><li>Solderless Stacking Mezzanine Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector market encompasses several types of connector technologies. Through-hole technology involves inserting connector leads through holes in a printed circuit board, ensuring robust mechanical support. Surface-mount technology allows connectors to be mounted directly on the board surface, enabling compact designs and automated assembly. Plug-in technology enables easy interconnection of boards through standard connectors. Solderless stacking mezzanine technology allows boards to be stacked without soldering, facilitating modular configurations and simplifying maintenance and upgrades.</p></p>
