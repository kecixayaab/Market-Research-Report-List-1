<p><ul><li>Lead-Acid Micro-grid ESS</li><li>Li-ion Micro-grid ESS</li><li>Saltwater Micro-grid ESS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro-grid Energy Storage Systems (ESS) include various market types based on battery technologies. Lead-Acid Micro-grid ESS is traditionally cost-effective but has shorter lifespans and lower energy density. Li-Ion Micro-grid ESS offers higher efficiency, longer cycle life, and rapid deployment, making it increasingly popular. Saltwater Micro-grid ESS, an emerging option, utilizes non-toxic materials and is environmentally friendly, although it currently lacks the maturity and widespread adoption of the other technologies. Each type serves unique energy storage needs within micro-grid applications.</p></p>
