<p><p>The semiconductor fabrication chemicals market is categorized into three main types: gas, solid, and liquid chemicals. Gas chemicals, such as silane and hydrogen fluoride, are crucial for processes like chemical vapor deposition. Solid chemicals, including photoresists and etchants, are used in lithography and etching processes. Liquid chemicals primarily encompass cleaning agents and chemical developers that maintain equipment and structures during fabrication. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring precision and efficiency in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
