<p><ul><li>Passive Smart Thermal Control System</li><li>Active Smart Thermal Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Thermal Control System market includes two main types: Passive and Active Smart Thermal Control Systems. Passive systems utilize intrinsic materials and design features to naturally regulate temperature without needing external power, relying on thermal insulation and reflective surfaces. In contrast, Active systems employ sensors, actuators, and control algorithms to dynamically manage temperature, making adjustments based on real-time data. These systems integrate advanced technologies for improved efficiency, comfort, and energy savings in various applications such as buildings and vehicles.</p></p>
