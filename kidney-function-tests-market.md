<p><p>The kidney function tests market encompasses various assessments to evaluate renal health. Dilution and concentration tests measure the kidney's ability to concentrate urine, reflecting its functional capacity. Clearance tests determine the efficiency of the kidneys in filtering substances from the blood, often using creatinine as a marker. Urine tests analyze the composition and presence of proteins, electrolytes, or other substances, providing insights into kidney function. Imaging tests utilize techniques like ultrasound or CT scans to visualize kidney structure and function, enhancing diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
