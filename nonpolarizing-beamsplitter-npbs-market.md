The market for NPBS is divided into two primary types: narrowband and broadband. Narrowband NPBS are designed for specific wavelength ranges, ensuring high precision in applications like spectroscopy. In contrast, broadband NPBS operate over a wider spectrum of wavelengths, making them suitable for diverse uses in general optics, imaging systems, and laser applications.</p></p>
