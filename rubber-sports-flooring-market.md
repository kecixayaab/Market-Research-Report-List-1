<p><p>The rubber sports flooring market comprises two main types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber is derived from latex harvested from rubber trees, offering excellent elasticity, durability, and comfort, making it ideal for high-impact sports surfaces. Synthetic rubber, on the other hand, is manufactured using chemical processes, providing versatility in design and greater resistance to wear, moisture, and UV exposure. Both types cater to varied preferences in performance, aesthetics, and environmental considerations within the sports flooring industry.</p></p>
