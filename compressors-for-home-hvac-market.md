<p><p>In the home HVAC market, compressors are crucial for efficient cooling and heating. Screw compressors use two interlocking screws for steady airflow and high efficiency, while rotary compressors utilize a rotating mechanism for compact designs in small spaces. Centrifugal compressors rely on high-speed rotating impellers, ideal for larger systems. Scroll compressors consist of two spirals that compress refrigerant quietly and efficiently, and reciprocating compressors use moving pistons, providing reliable performance for various heating and cooling applications.</p></p>
