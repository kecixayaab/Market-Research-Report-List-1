<p><p>The electric scooter battery market features several key types: Lead-Acid, Lithium-Ion, and Lithium Phosphate batteries. Lead-Acid batteries are cost-effective but heavier and have a shorter lifespan. Lithium-Ion batteries are lightweight, offer higher energy density, and improved longevity, making them popular in modern scooters. Lithium Phosphate batteries, a subtype of lithium batteries, provide enhanced thermal stability and safety, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles. Each battery type caters to different consumer needs based on cost, weight, and performance criteria.</p></p>
