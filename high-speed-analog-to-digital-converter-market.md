<p><p>The high-speed analog-to-digital converter (ADC) market is segmented into single-channel and dual-channel types. Single-channel ADCs process one input signal at a time, making them suitable for applications requiring simplicity and lower costs. In contrast, dual-channel ADCs can handle two signals simultaneously, enhancing data acquisition efficiency and reducing system complexity in applications like communications and imaging. Both types cater to varying application needs, with single-channel favored for straightforward tasks and dual-channel for more demanding, multi-signal scenarios.</p></p>
