<p><p>Bakelite is categorized into three primary market types: alcohol-soluble, modified, and oil-soluble. Alcohol-soluble Bakelite is primarily used in adhesives and coatings due to its excellent film-forming properties. Modified Bakelite, enhanced with various additives, improves performance characteristics, making it suitable for higher-end applications like electrical components. Oil-soluble Bakelite, designed for use in oil-based formulations, is ideal for lubricants and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs while capitalizing on the versatile properties of Bakelite.</p></p>
