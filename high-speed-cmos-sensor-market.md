<p><strong>The High Speed CMOS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS</li><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High-Speed CMOS Sensor market encompasses two main types: Front-Illuminated (FI) CMOS and Backside-Illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensors. FI CMOS sensors, while traditionally used, often struggle with light sensitivity and noise. In contrast, BSI CMOS sensors are engineered with a structure that captures more light, significantly enhancing image quality in low-light conditions. This makes BSI more suitable for high-speed applications such as video recording and advanced imaging, driving its increasing adoption in various industries, including consumer electronics and automotive.</p></p>
