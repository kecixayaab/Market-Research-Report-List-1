<p><p>The measles vaccine market includes two primary products: MMR-II and Proquad. MMR-II is a live vaccine that protects against measles, mumps, and rubella, essential for preventing outbreaks and ensuring community immunity. Proquad combines MMR with varicella (chickenpox) vaccination, providing a dual protection in a single dose, ideal for comprehensive immunization. Both vaccines are crucial in public health efforts to reduce incidences of these diseases, particularly in children, and are administered through routine childhood vaccination schedules.</p></p>
