<p><p>The Tip Location Devices market includes three primary types: ECG Tip Confirmation, which utilizes electrocardiographic signals to ensure accurate catheter placement; ECG with Magnetic Tracking, which combines ECG data with magnetic fields for real-time location tracking of catheters; and ECG and Intravascular Doppler, which integrates ECG capabilities with Doppler ultrasound to assess blood flow dynamics while confirming catheter tip positions. These technologies enhance procedural accuracy and patient safety in various medical applications, particularly in cardiology and interventional procedures.</p></p>
