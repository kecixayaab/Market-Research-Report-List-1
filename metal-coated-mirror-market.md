<p><strong>The Metal-Coated Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>First Surface Mirror</li><li>Second Surface Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal-coated mirror market is categorized into first surface mirrors and second surface mirrors. First surface mirrors feature the reflective coating applied directly to the front surface, eliminating distortion and providing optimal reflectivity, making them ideal for high-precision applications. In contrast, second surface mirrors have the coating applied to the back, resulting in some light absorption and potential distortion, which may be suitable for general applications. Both types serve distinct purposes across various industries, including optics, automotive, and decorative uses.</p></p>
