<p><strong>The MEMS Ink Jet Print Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Print Head</li><li>Piezo Print Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS Ink Jet Print Head market is primarily divided into two types: Thermal Print Heads and Piezo Print Heads. Thermal Print Heads use heat to create bubbles in ink, propelling droplets onto the substrate, making them suitable for high-speed printing. Piezo Print Heads utilize mechanical pressure from piezoelectric materials to eject ink, allowing precise droplet control and a broader range of inks. Both technologies cater to varying applications in consumer and industrial printing, influencing market growth and innovation.</p></p>
