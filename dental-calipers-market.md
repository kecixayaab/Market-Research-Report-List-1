<p><p>The dental calipers market comprises primarily two types: straight dental calipers and dial dental calipers. Straight dental calipers, characterized by their elongated, straight design, are used for precise measurements of tooth dimensions and gaps. Dial dental calipers, on the other hand, incorporate a dial gauge that provides enhanced accuracy and ease of reading measurements, making them ideal for detailed assessments. Both types are essential tools in dentistry, facilitating accurate diagnostics and treatment planning.</p></p>
