<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile C-arm X-ray tubes market is primarily divided into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode. Stationary anode tubes feature a fixed target that provides stability and is typically used for applications requiring less intense radiation. In contrast, rotating anode tubes have a rotating target that allows for higher heat dissipation, enabling prolonged usage and greater imaging quality. The choice between these types depends on the specific imaging requirements and operational demands of healthcare facilities.</p></p>
